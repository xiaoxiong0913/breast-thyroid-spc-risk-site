# Figure Legends

## Figure 1
Cohort derivation flow for the month-level SEER analysis. The diagram summarizes the SEER case-listing source, MP-SIR latency supplement, patient-level linkage, derivation of the first malignant breast-cancer index cohort, follow-up eligibility, and calendar-based allocation to development and temporal-validation cohorts.

## Figure 2
Temporal discrimination across machine-learning models and benchmark comparators. Panel A shows temporal receiver operating characteristic curves with AUROC estimates. Panel B shows temporal precision-recall curves for the same comparison set.

## Figure 3
Feature screening and model-feature review. The figure presents LASSO screening, collinearity review, and feature-importance information used to summarize the retained clinicopathologic predictor set.

## Figure 4
Temporal calibration and decision-curve analysis for the selected CatBoost model. Panel A summarizes calibration across locked risk bands. Panel B shows temporal decision-curve analysis against treat-all and treat-none reference strategies.

## Figure 5
Youden-anchored temporal risk-group presentation. Panel A shows mean predicted 5-year risk across low-, intermediate-, and high-risk groups. Panel B shows the corresponding observed 5-year event rates with event counts.

## Figure 6
SHAP summary of the selected CatBoost model. The figure summarizes global feature contribution patterns using the locked SHAP color scheme.

## Figure 7
Interactive public web calculator. The figure shows the Render-hosted page that accepts the retained clinicopathologic predictors and returns the calibrated 5-year predicted risk and locked risk group. The public URL is https://breast-thyroid-spc-risk-site.onrender.com.
